# Create Your Account

Getting started with Frequency Digital is quick and easy. Follow these steps to create your account:

## Step 1: Visit the Sign Up Page

Navigate to our registration page and click "Get Started".

## Step 2: Enter Your Details

- **Email Address**: Use a valid email you have access to
- **Password**: Create a strong password with at least 8 characters
- **Music Role**: Select whether you're an artist, label, or manager

## Step 3: Verify Your Email

Check your inbox for a verification email and click the confirmation link.

## Step 4: Complete Your Profile

Add some basic information to personalize your experience.

That's it! You're ready to start tracking your music analytics.

# Connect Your Music Platforms

Link all your streaming accounts to get comprehensive analytics across platforms.

## Supported Platforms

- **Spotify for Artists**
- **Apple Music for Artists**
- **YouTube Music**
- **Amazon Music**
- **SoundCloud**
- **Tidal**

## How to Connect

1. Go to **Settings** > **Connected Platforms**
2. Click **Add Platform**
3. Select the platform you want to connect
4. Authorize the connection through the platform's secure login
5. Repeat for additional platforms

## Security Note

We use OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication. We never store your passwords—only secure tokens provided by each platform.

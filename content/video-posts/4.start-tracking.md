# Start Tracking

Now that you're set up, it's time to explore your analytics dashboard.

## Dashboard Overview

Your main dashboard shows:

Earnings broken down by platform and time period.

### Audience Growth

Track follower counts and engagement metrics.

### Geographic Data

See where your listeners are located around the world.

## Key Features

- **Real-time Updates**: Data refreshes every hour
- **Historical Comparisons**: Compare performance over time
- **Export Reports**: Download CSV reports for your records
- **Alert Notifications**: Get notified of milestones and anomalies

## Tips for Success

1. Check your dashboard weekly
2. Set goals based on your current metrics
3. Use geographic data to plan tours or marketing
4. Track which releases perform best

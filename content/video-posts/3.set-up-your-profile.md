# Set Up Your Profile

Personalize your Frequency Digital experience by completing your profile.

## Profile Elements

### Artist Name
Your stage name or the name you release music under.

### Bio
Tell your story! A brief description of who you are and your musical journey.

### Profile Picture
Upload a high-quality image that represents you or your brand.

### Social Links
Connect your social media accounts:
- Instagram
- Twitter/X
- Facebook
- TikTok
- YouTube Channel

## Why Complete Your Profile?

A complete profile helps us:
- Customize your dashboard
- Provide relevant insights
- Connect you with opportunities
- Improve your discoverability
